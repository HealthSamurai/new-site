Integration of EHR into Custom Application

We were contacted by a team developing a web service to collect feedbacks from patients receiving treatment in clinics. The web service handles data from EHRs, on top of which it is deployed in clinics. It's important for the service to receive information about patient registration, transfers within a clinic, statements of diagnostic or therapeutical procedures fulfilment.

The developer company did not have experience in integration and knowledge of standards, therefore they contacted us and offered the Technology Partnership (regarding the integration). To confirm the working hypothesis, we implemented a proof of concept. We created an integration module that receives a flow of HL7 v2.5.1 data regarding registrations (ADT), transfers (ADT) and performed diagnostic procedures (ORU), handles it and forms a control command for the service of feedback collection, by which this service launches a mechanism of feedback collection.
